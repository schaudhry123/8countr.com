About 8 Count

We are creating a small adhesive strip you put on your mouth guard to detect concussions.

We are based in Urbana, Champaign (map courtsey of Snazzy Maps.

8 Count will revolutionize the sports world, providing an immediate method to determine the danger of players and athletes as they compete.

How We Accomplish This

8 Count will track the linear and rotational acceleration of the player through a chip in the adhesvie strip, determinining the probability of a concussion from a certain hit. This will be displayed on our mobile app on an easy-to-use interface that notifies the user of any potentially dangerous hits.
